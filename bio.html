About Me

Finn Ominal is a music producer, storyteller, and creative force born in Flatbush, Brooklyn. With over 20 years of experience, he brings a versatile sound that fuses Hip Hop, R&B, Dancehall, Soca, Reggae, Afrobeats, Amapiano, and cinematic film scores.

One half of the production duo Urban MindState and a member of the East Side City Music collective, Finn is driven by collaboration and cultural storytelling. His foundation in music production and audio engineering, sharpened at Full Sail University, fuels both his sonic craft and the message behind it.

Outside the studio, Finn is also a graphic designer, video editor, and hands-on creative. Whether it’s sound, visuals, or DIY craftwork, his work is rooted in identity, emotion, and purpose.

Mission: To give his family a life of freedom, love, and laughter — and to connect with others through truth and sound.

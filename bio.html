About Finn Ominal

Finn Ominal is a music producer, storyteller, and creative force born in Flatbush, Brooklyn. With over 20 years of experience, he fuses Hip Hop, R&B, Dancehall, Soca, Reggae, Afrobeats, Amapiano, and cinematic scores. His sound is raw, real, and rooted in connection.

He’s one half of the production duo Urban MindState, alongside Muzique By Blow, and a proud member of the East Side City Music collective. Whether producing behind the scenes or leading a creative vision, Finn’s work is always driven by emotion, culture, and purpose.

Outside of music, Finn is a skilled graphic designer, video editor, and hands-on creative. Every project reflects his mission to uplift his family and reach others with authentic, powerful storytelling.

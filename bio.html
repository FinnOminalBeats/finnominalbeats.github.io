About Finn Ominal

Music producer, storyteller, and creative force born in Flatbush, Brooklyn.

With over 20 years of experience and a deep passion for music creation, Finn Ominal brings a versatile sound that fuses Hip Hop, R&B, Dancehall, Soca, Reggae, Afrobeats, Amapiano, and cinematic film scores.

He is one half of the production duo Urban MindState, alongside Muzique By Blow, and a member of the East Side City Music collective, where cultural storytelling takes center stage.

His foundation in music production and audio engineering was sharpened at Full Sail University, fueling his commitment to both craft and message.

Outside the studio, Finn is also a graphic designer, video editor, and DIY crafter, bringing his storytelling skills into every medium.

At the heart of it all, his mission is clear: to give his family a life of freedom, love, and laughter — and to reach others through music that speaks truth and shares culture.

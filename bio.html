About Finn Ominal

Finn Ominal is a music producer, storyteller, and creative force born in Flatbush, Brooklyn. With over 20 years of experience and a deep passion for music creation, he brings a versatile sound that fuses Hip Hop, R&B, Dancehall, Soca, Reggae, Afrobeats, Amapiano, and cinematic film scores.

He is one half of the production duo Urban MindState, alongside Muzique By Blow, and a member of the East Side City Music collective, where collaboration and cultural storytelling are front and center.

A Full Sail University grad in music production and audio engineering, Finn blends technical excellence with emotional storytelling. Currently, he's producing a bold new wave of releases, including Divine Times by Li63rty — launching Dec 26, 2025.

Outside the studio, Finn is also a designer, video editor, and DIY craftsman. Whether it’s sound, visuals, or hands-on projects, his art is grounded in emotion, identity, and purpose — all rooted in the dream of creating freedom, love, and legacy for his family.
